Christmas Riddle

An evening awaits, secret and fine,
but only a riddle will give you the sign.

Waves and warmth, architecture unique,
a place of calm, the rest we seek.
There we’ll unwind, just you and me,
the answer? Found poetically.

But this day is more than just a date,
the 27th holds a special weight.
A moment to cherish, tender and true,
it’s the start of something new.

Then comes delight, a meal to savor,
a table set with taste and favor.
Together, with time that’s just our own,
will you solve the path that’s shown?

Take the first letter of each clue,
and you’ll discover what’s waiting for you.

Have fun solving the riddle!

It’s time.
